📖 Sample Glossary

  • Bull Market: A market in which prices are rising or expected to rise.
  • Bear Market: A market in which prices are falling or expected to fall.
  • IPO: Initial Public Offering, when a company first sells shares to the public.
  • Dividend: A portion of a company's earnings paid to shareholders.
  • Portfolio: A collection of financial investments like stocks, bonds, etc.
